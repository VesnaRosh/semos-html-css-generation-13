How to Apply Face Cream
  1. Applying Face Cream
    1. Wash your hands
    2. Clean your face
      1. Splash your face with cold water and gently pat it dry with a soft towel
      2. Consider applying some toner
        1. If you have dry or sensitive skin, opt for an alcohol-free toner
        2. Apply eye cream
          1. place a tiny amount onto your finger
          2. Avoid pulling on the skin under your eyes
          3. Gently dab the cream under your eyes
        3. Apply face cream
        4. Apply more cream if necessary
      3. Apply face cream to your neck
        1. The skin on your neck is delicate
        2. It needs some attention as well
      4. Choose a face cream
    3. Pay attention to the season
      1. If your skin is dry-moisturizing face cream
      2. If your skin is oily-light-weight moisturizing gel.
    4. Consider using a tinted moisturizer
  2. Know that even oily skin needs face cream
    1. Look for face creams that say they are meant for oily skin (or acne) on the label.
      1. Opt for a light-weight, moisturizing gel instead.
      2. Consider getting a matte-finish cream
        1. It will help reduce shine
        2. It will make your skin appear less oily.
    2. Choose rich, hydrating creams if you have dry skin
    3. Look for products that say they are intended for dry skin
    4. Look for labels that say "hydrating" or "moisturizing."
  3. Look for gentle creams if you have sensitive skin
